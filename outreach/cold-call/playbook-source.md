# Corgi Cold Call Playbook — Source Reference

Saved from PDF: cold_call_GPU_RV (19 March 2026)

Core positioning: Corgi helps GPU owners and their lenders get a better debt structure by reducing residual value risk on the hardware. Lead with capital efficiency, not insurance.

## Buyer Personas
1. **Data center / GPU owner** — pain: hard to finance GPUs because lenders worry about 3-5yr value
2. **Lender / private credit / equipment finance** — pain: residual value hard to underwrite at maturity
3. **Structured capital partner / debt arranger** — pain: deals slow when no one takes RV risk

## Scripts: A (cheaper capital), B (better debt structure), C (lender angle), D (operator pain), E (simple CTA)

## Goal of first call: get interest for a founder call with Isaac and Josh. Don't explain the full policy.

## Words to use: cost of capital, debt structure, collateral, downside protection, underwriting confidence, residual value floor
## Words to avoid: reinsurance structure, Cayman, Lloyd's mechanics, captive details, policy wording, put option jargon

## 20 target companies: 8 Priority A operators, 4 Priority B operators, 3 Priority B lenders, 5 Priority C lenders
