## General Tips — All Calls

1. **Lead with their debt structure**, not the insurance product. RVG is a financing tool, not a risk product.
2. **Never say "hedge depreciation"** — say "lower your cost of capital." Depreciation framing makes it sound like the GPUs are dying. Cost-of-capital framing makes it sound like smart finance.
3. **The lender angle is your leverage.** If the operator hesitates, ask: "Would it help if we approached your lending syndicate directly? They tend to be very receptive."
4. **Per-cohort, per-facility** — don't let them think they need to insure everything at once. Start with one GPU cluster or one debt tranche.
5. **SiliconNavigator benchmarks** — mention this. Transparent, daily GPU pricing removes the "how do you even price this?" objection.
6. **Title transfer** — always mention this early. It eliminates the moral hazard concern that insurance people worry about.
7. **Comparable industries** — auto (RVI), aircraft (RVG), heavy equipment. This isn't new, it's just new to GPUs.
