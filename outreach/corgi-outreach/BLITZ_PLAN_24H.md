## BOOKING MECHANICS — HOW TO GET A YES

**Never say: "Would you be interested in learning more?"**  
This is a passive question that gets passive responses.

**Instead, use time-anchored options:**
> "I have two slots available this week: Tuesday 3pm ET or Wednesday 11am ET. Which works better for a 20-minute intro?"

**Or for the calendar-drop:**
> "I'll send you a Calendly link — it has 20-minute slots available starting Monday. Looking forward to it."

**The 5-minute hook offer:**
> "If Tuesday doesn't work, I can do a 5-minute WhatsApp voice note that covers the entire product — no calendar needed. Just say yes and I'll record it now."

**Objection: "Send me some materials first."**
✅ DO THIS: "Absolutely — sending one-pager now. I'll follow up in 48 hours to answer questions. Best time to catch you for a quick follow-up call?"
