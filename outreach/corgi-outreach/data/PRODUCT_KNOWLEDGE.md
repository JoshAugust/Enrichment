# Corgi Product Knowledge Base — GPU Residual Value Guaranty (RVG)

## What Corgi Does
Corgi Insurance Services provides **Residual Value Guaranty (RVG) insurance for GPU hardware** used in data centers. It's essentially a put option on GPU value — guaranteeing the hardware maintains a minimum percentage of its original value at a specified future date. If the GPU's market value drops below the guaranteed floor, the policy pays out.

## How It Works
1. **GPU owner purchases RVG policy** at time of GPU acquisition
2. **Selects guarantee level** (5%, 10%, or 15% of original GPU value) and **term** (3, 5, or 10 years)
3. **Pays one-time premium** (2.1% to 12.9% of GPU value depending on tier)
4. **At policy expiration**: if market value < guaranteed floor → full payout + title transfer to carrier
5. **Title transfer eliminates moral hazard** — insured gives up the hardware to collect
