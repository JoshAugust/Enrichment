## First Mover — No Standalone GPU RVG Exists

A purpose-built, standalone GPU Residual Value Guarantee product sold to GPU **lenders** to protect collateral floors at loan maturity **does not currently exist as a commercial product.** The GPU ABS market has used RVI structurally (ad hoc, within specific deals) but no insurer offers this as a standard lender product.

This is the same white space RVI Group occupied in 1989. **Corgi has first-mover advantage.**

## The Aircraft RVI Analog (Your Credibility Story)

> *"RVI Group didn't have 30 years of aircraft data in 1989 either. They built the market."*
