## 🇸🇬 Singapore

### 1. Sustainable Metal Cloud (SMC)
- **What:** Singapore's most energy-efficient GPU cloud; hyperscale A100, L40, H100 clusters in Singapore and India. Liquid-cooled at scale. Ranked alongside AWS by SemiAnalysis.
- **GPU Fleet / Funding:** Multi-thousand GPU fleet across SG and India; est. $50M–$100M in hardware
- **Key Contact:** CEO — Benny Har-Even (reach via [smc.co](https://smc.co))

### 2. GMI Cloud (Singapore HQ)
- **What:** NVIDIA Reference Cloud Platform Provider. H100/H200 bare metal and serverless inference. HQ at 1 Raffles Place, Singapore.
- **GPU Fleet / Funding:** Thousands of H100/H200 GPUs globally; 30–50% cheaper than hyperscalers
- **Key Contact:** Reach via [gmicloud.ai](https://www.gmicloud.ai)

### 3. Singtel (Sovereign AI)
- **What:** Building energy-efficient NVIDIA Hopper-powered data centers across Southeast Asia as sovereign national AI factories for governments, enterprises, and startups.
- **GPU Fleet / Funding:** Major telco capex; NVIDIA partnership for sovereign AI; est. $100M+ in GPU infrastructure
- **Key Contact:** Group CEO — Yuen Kuan Moon

### 4. Aethir (Decentralized GPU Cloud)
- **What:** Decentralized GPU cloud operating 435,000+ GPU containers globally, significant SG presence. NVIDIA H100s at ~86% cheaper than AWS.
- **GPU Fleet / Funding:** 435K+ GPU containers; est. $100M+ in distributed hardware
- **Key Contact:** CEO — Mark Rydon

- **GPU Fleet / Funding:** ~10,800 GPUs (H200 + B200); est. $200M–$300M+ in hardware; ¥50.1B (~$340M) METI subsidy
- **Key Contact:** CEO — Kunihiro Tanaka ([sakura.ad.jp](https://www.sakura.ad.jp))

### 6. Preferred Networks (PFN)
- **What:** Japan's premier AI research company; operates MN-3/MN-Core custom supercomputers; GPU-heavy infrastructure for industrial AI (Toyota, FANUC partnerships).
- **GPU Fleet / Funding:** Custom MN-Core chips + NVIDIA GPUs; est. $100M+ in compute hardware
- **Key Contact:** CEO — Toru Nishikawa

- **Key Contact:** CEO — Ryu Young-sang
