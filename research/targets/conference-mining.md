- **Keynote-featured companies:** Capital One, Rivian, Meta (Yann LeCun), Caltech (Frances Arnold)

### Notable Speakers (2025)

- Yann LeCun (Meta, Chief AI Scientist)

- **Third-party attendee lists:** Vendelux (vendelux.com) sells curated GTC attendee/exhibitor data

### GPU Risk Relevance
- **HIGH.** Exhibitors are GPU buyers, deployers, and integrators. Infrastructure companies here are prime insurance targets for GPU asset coverage, data center property, and business interruption.

## Cross-Cutting: How to Find Speaker/Attendee Contact Info

### Strategies That Work
1. **LinkedIn Search:** Search "[Conference Name] [Year] speaker" — most speakers announce their talks. Connect with a personalized note referencing their talk.
2. **Conference Session Catalogs:** Almost all conferences publish speaker name + company + talk title. Cross-reference with LinkedIn.
3. **OpenReview / IEEE / ACM:** Academic conferences publish full author lists with email affiliations.
4. **Vendelux (vendelux.com):** Commercial platform that aggregates conference attendee data — sells targeted lists for GTC, RISKWORLD, SC, OCP, etc.
5. **Company Press Releases:** Companies issue "visit us at booth #X" press releases — usually name the executives attending.
6. **Event Apps:** Most major conferences have mobile apps with attendee directories (requires registration).
7. **Twitter/X:** Search event hashtags during/after conferences — speakers and attendees share photos, talks, and booth visits.
8. **YouTube:** Conference talk recordings often include speaker intros with titles and contact details.
9. **Artemis.bm / Reinsurance News** (for insurance conferences): Cover who said what — name executives directly.
10. **Sponsor Prospectuses:** Many conferences publish sponsor/exhibitor prospectuses (PDFs) that list previous sponsors with logos — use for company identification.

### Tools for Contact Enrichment
- **Apollo.io** — find emails/phones from company + name
- **Hunter.io** — email finder by domain
- **Lusha** — B2B contact data
- **ZoomInfo** — enterprise contact database
- **LinkedIn Sales Navigator** — advanced people search with conference activity filters
