### 🇿🇦 South Africa

#### CSIR CHPC (Centre for High Performance Computing)
- **Country:** South Africa (Cape Town / national)
- **GPU Operations:** Lengau cluster: 30× NVIDIA V100 GPUs (6 nodes × 3 + 3 nodes × 4). New 4-petaFLOP system purchased (R400M), 10 racks, direct water cooling — ~4× more powerful than Lengau. AI cluster for training/inference.
- **Scale:** Research/national HPC. Not commercial GPU rental at scale.
- **Notes:** Operated by NICIS (CSIR division). Free to academic users. Commercial: R0.45+VAT per core-hour. Quantum computing access launching. Serves 2,000+ users.
- **Contact:** Web: chpc.ac.za | info@chpc.ac.za

#### Teraco Data Environments
- **Country:** South Africa (Johannesburg primary)
- **GPU Operations:** JB5 (30 MW): first at-scale AI workload facility, GPUs used by hyperscalers. JB4 Bredell (50 MW): Africa's largest standalone DC; liquid-to-liquid cooling for AI. JB7 expansion: R8B committed, +40 MW AI-ready. Altron AI Factory hosted at Teraco (NVIDIA DGX-Ready partner).
- **Scale:** 120+ MW capacity across campuses. AI GPU density increasing — JB5 and JB7 specifically AI-ready.
- **Notes:** Africa's dominant colocation operator. NVIDIA DGX-Ready programme participant. Not a GPU cloud provider per se — more colocation + GPU-ready infrastructure for enterprise tenants.
- **Contact:** Web: teraco.co.za

#### Cassava Technologies AI Factory — Johannesburg

## KEY THEMES

1. **NVIDIA dominance** — virtually every significant GPU operator across all these markets is in the NVIDIA ecosystem (H100, B200, DGX). AMD presence minimal (Jio has 268 MI300X, exception rather than rule).
2. **Sovereign AI framing** — universal. Every operator uses "data sovereignty" language. Regulatory tailwind for local GPU deployments.
3. **Telcos as GPU operators** — Safaricom, Jio, Telkom Indonesia, Viettel, Singtel all deploying or anchoring GPU infrastructure. Different from the Western hyperscaler model.
4. **Power is the binding constraint in Africa** — Nigeria's grid (<6 GW for 230M people) is the single biggest limiter. Kenya's geothermal and Morocco's solar/wind are meaningful advantages.
5. **India is the most advanced** — IndiaAI Mission is sophisticated, well-funded, multi-vendor. Yotta is the bellwether.
6. **Southeast Asia is fragmented but moving fast** — Singapore (quality), Indonesia (scale), Vietnam (sovereignty push) each playing different angles.
