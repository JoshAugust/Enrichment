## Key Structural Insights

1. **SPV model dominates:** Special Purpose Vehicle acquires GPUs → leases back to AI company. Title stays with SPV, AI company gets compute access without balance-sheet hit.
2. **Triple-net lease:** Apollo/xAI deals use this structure — lessee responsible for all operating costs.
3. **Collateral = GPUs + customer contracts:** Lenders take security over both the physical chips and the revenue contracts.
4. **14% average interest rate** — ~3x investment-grade corporate debt.
5. **Depreciation risk is the #1 concern:** 30–40% year-one depreciation. Lenders advance 50–70% of GPU value.
6. **Concentration risk:** CoreWeave's 62% of revenue from Microsoft is a red flag for lenders.
7. **Refinancing wall:** CoreWeave faces $986M due 2025, $4.2B in 2026.
