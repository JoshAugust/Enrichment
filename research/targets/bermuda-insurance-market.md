# Bermuda Insurance/Reinsurance Market — P&C Specialty Lines
**Research Date:** March 2026  
**Sources:** ABIR member list, AM Best rankings, BMA filings, company reports, industry press  
**Purpose:** Identify Bermuda-based carriers writing P&C specialty lines, especially tech asset / residual value / equipment coverage

---

## Market Overview

- **Total Bermuda market:** 1,200+ (re)insurers; total assets > $1.6 trillion; GWP ≈ $268 billion
- **ABIR member GWP (2024):** >$188 billion; equity >$178 billion
- **Bermuda P&C sector net income (2024):** $30.9 billion (+22.5% YoY)
- **Bermuda P&C net written premiums (2024):** $72.6 billion (+5.4% YoY)
- **New registrations (2024):** 63 new insurers registered with the BMA
- **BMA Classes for P&C commercial:** Classes 3A, 3B (≥$50M net premiums from unrelated business), Class 4 (excess liability / cat re, min $100M capital)

## Key Observations for Outreach

1. **RVI is a niche product** — only a small number of global carriers write it. RVI Group (Bermuda-based) is the dominant specialist and almost certainly uses Lloyd's and Bermuda market capacity behind it. Getting to their capacity providers is the key.

2. **Fidelis' "asset-backed finance" line** is the most explicit product among large Bermuda carriers that maps to equipment/residual value financing. Direct outreach to Richard Brindle or Hannah Greenwood is warranted.

3. **Hannover Re Bermuda's Financial Solutions unit** (led by Chantal Cardinez) is specifically structured to write bespoke/structured (re)insurance transactions — including asset-backed structures. They are accustomed to building products around unusual asset exposures.

4. **AXIS Capital** appointed a dedicated Cyber & Technology CUO in 2024 — this is a major signal. They're building out this book and may be receptive to new product discussions.

5. **The "Class of 2020/2021" startups** (Conduit Re, Mosaic, Relm) are generally more receptive to novel risk structures than the established majors. Mosaic (credit, surety, transactional) and Relm (emerging tech) are worth early conversations.

6. **Bermuda market structure:** Most placement goes via London/Bermuda brokers. The key Bermuda broker intermediaries are Aon, Gallagher Re, Lockton Re, and Marsh McLennan/Guy Carpenter — all are ABIR Broker Advisory Cabinet members. Any Bermuda market approach should involve one of these brokers.
