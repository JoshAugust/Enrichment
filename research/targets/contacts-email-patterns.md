## NOTES FOR OUTREACH

1. **Most common pattern across all companies:** `first.last@domain.com` — safe default assumption when crafting outreach
2. **Exceptions to watch:**
   - Together AI uses `firstname@` only
   - Crusoe, Arch Capital, Ares IR use `flast@` (first initial + last)
   - RenaissanceRe has legacy `initials@` for senior staff
   - Blackstone uses `First.Last@` with **Title Case** (unusual)
3. **Applied Digital has no direct company press email** — uses agencies (JSA, Gateway Group)
4. **Awbury is the most transparent** — full contact page with individual emails per office per entity
5. **Convex and Fidelis rely on external PR agencies** for media — go direct to IR contacts for more reliable routing
6. **Iris Energy domain is .co not .com** — important to note for deliverability
