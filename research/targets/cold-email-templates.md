3. **Personalization minimum:** Always customize `{{company_name}}`, `{{first_name}}`, and at least one business-specific detail

7. **GDPR note:** For EU/UK recipients, ensure legitimate interest basis is documented; consider adding consent-based opt-in for ongoing communications.
