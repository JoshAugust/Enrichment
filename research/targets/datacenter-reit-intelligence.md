## Why Data Center REITs Matter for GPU Insurance Distribution

Data center landlords have **direct visibility** into who is deploying GPUs at scale. They know:
- Which tenants are drawing high-density power (a proxy for GPU deployment)
- The MW committed and growth trajectory of each AI tenant
- Creditworthiness and contract duration of GPU operators
- When tenants are expanding (trigger for new GPU insurance needs)

**Distribution play:** Partner with REIT leasing/sales teams to bundle GPU RVG insurance into new tenant onboarding or capacity expansion deals. The REIT gets a value-add for tenants; we get warm introductions to every GPU deployer on their platform.
