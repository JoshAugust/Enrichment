### Key Framing for Conversations
- **Asset class:** High-value technology infrastructure (GPU accelerators / AI compute hardware)
- **Risk:** Residual value decline due to technological obsolescence, market oversupply, or generation shifts
- **Structure:** Residual value guarantee — payout if fair market value at lease/contract end falls below agreed floor
- **Analogues:** Equipment leasing RVI (construction, medical, aviation), auto RVG programmes
- **Data advantage:** GPU depreciation curves are trackable (NVIDIA product cycles, secondary market pricing, utilization metrics)
- **Reinsurance need:** Treaty or facultative capacity behind an MGA or programme carrier writing GPU RVG policies
