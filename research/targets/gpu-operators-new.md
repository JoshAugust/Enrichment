## Key Themes for Sales Positioning

1. **GPU-as-collateral is now a $20B+ asset class** — lenders (Blackstone, PIMCO, Blue Owl, JPMorgan, Macquarie) are demanding better risk management
2. **Depreciation risk is real** — new GPU generations (H100 → H200 → Blackwell → next) compress residual value windows
3. **European sovereign AI mandates** (Nscale, DataCrunch, NexGen, Ori) create regulatory pressure for asset protection
4. **Debt covenants often require insurance** — position residual value insurance as enabling better loan terms
5. **The "neocloud default risk" narrative** is growing — Medium/analyst pieces questioning whether GPU debt is sustainable. Insurance is the answer.
