# NVIDIA Partner Ecosystem Research
**Research Date:** March 2026  
**Sources:** NVIDIA.com partner pages, NPN awards blogs, press releases, public filings, news reporting  

---

## Overview

NVIDIA distributes its GPU/AI platform through a tiered partner ecosystem called the **NVIDIA Partner Network (NPN)**. The highest tiers are:
- **Elite** – deepest commitment, maximum GPU revenue, full training/competency stack
- **Preferred** – mid-tier
- **Registered** – entry-level

Specializations (invitation-only): AI Factory, Agentic AI, Embedded AI, Physical AI, Reference Platform NCP

## Research Notes

- **NPN member count:** NVIDIA's 2024 Americas NPN awards were drawn from a pool of **350 channel partners** in North America alone; total NPN membership is in the thousands globally.
- **DGX-Ready certification:** As of 2025, **38 colocation organizations worldwide** are DGX-Ready certified, of which **10 have Liquid Cooled Ready** designation (required for rack-scale systems 300kW+).
- **DGX Cloud Lepton:** Announced May 2025 at COMPUTEX; 10 founding NCP partners offering NVIDIA Blackwell GPUs on demand marketplace.
- **Hyperscaler GPU spend:** Each of the big 4 hyperscalers deploying ~1,000 NVL72 racks (~72,000 Blackwell GPUs) per week as of May 2025 per NVIDIA guidance.
- **NVIDIA's market position:** 92% data center GPU market share in 2025; total accelerated computing TAM driven by $600B+ combined CapEx from largest 6 operators in 2026.
