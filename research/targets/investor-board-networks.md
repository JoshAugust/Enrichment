## Insurance / Reinsurance Adjacencies

### Direct Insurance/Reinsurance Connections Found: **Limited but notable**

1. **Blackstone** (CoreWeave debt) — Blackstone has significant insurance portfolio holdings through its acquisition of insurance assets (e.g., Allstate Life, Resolution Life relationships). As CoreWeave's debt provider, Blackstone is a bridge to insurance capital.

2. **Macquarie Group** (Lambda, FluidStack, Applied Digital) — Macquarie's infrastructure lending division treats GPUs like infrastructure assets (toll roads, pipelines). This **asset-based lending model is directly relevant to insurance/reinsurance capital** that already invests in infrastructure debt.

3. **Fidelity** (CoreWeave, Crusoe) — Manages insurance company investment portfolios. Potential bridge to insurance asset allocators.

4. **T. Rowe Price** (Crusoe) — Manages insurance float and pension assets.

5. **Franklin Templeton** (Crusoe) — Has insurance-focused investment strategies.

6. **PIMCO** (Nscale debt) — Major manager of insurance company fixed-income portfolios. Their involvement in GPU infrastructure debt signals insurance capital is already flowing into this asset class.

7. **StepStone Group** (Crusoe) — Manages capital for institutional LPs including insurance companies.

8. **Blue Owl** (Crusoe, Nscale) — Direct lending / alternative credit; insurance companies are significant LPs in Blue Owl funds.

9. **Mubadala** (Crusoe Series E co-lead) — Abu Dhabi sovereign wealth fund; has insurance/reinsurance exposure through portfolio companies.

### Key Insight
> **No direct insurance/reinsurance firm sits on any board.** However, the debt infrastructure around these companies (Blackstone, Macquarie, PIMCO, Blue Owl) is heavily funded by insurance capital. The warm path to these GPU operators likely runs through the debt/infrastructure lending side, not the equity side.
