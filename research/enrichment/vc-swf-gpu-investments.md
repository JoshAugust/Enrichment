## PART B — SOVEREIGN WEALTH FUND GPU INVESTMENTS

*Compiled: March 2026 | Sources: Web searches, public filings, press releases, fund annual reports*

> **Insurance note (global):** No SWF publicly mandates insurance on individual portfolio assets as a condition of investment at the fund level. Standard practice is that operating companies/JVs hold their own property, casualty, and business interruption insurance per jurisdictional requirements and lender covenants. Data center JVs (e.g. Equinix xScale) are structured with commercial real estate insurance embedded in the JV agreement. GPU hardware specifically is typically covered under IT asset / equipment breakdown policies held by the datacenter operator. No SWF surveyed here has disclosed a fund-level GPU insurance mandate; this mirrors VC practice across Part A.
