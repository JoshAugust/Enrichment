## ⚠️ KEY MARKET INTELLIGENCE NOTES

1. **GPU failure rates 2–3% annually** — On a 10,000-GPU cluster at $35K avg, that's $7–10.5M in annual equipment losses.
2. **Liquid cooling is a new risk vector** — CDU (coolant distribution unit) failures and coolant leaks create new coverage gaps; most existing policies not written for this.
3. **Property policies exclude electronic data loss** — Major gap for AI companies whose revenue depends on model weights and training data.
4. **Triple net GPU leases** (Apollo/VCI/xAI structure) — Lessee (xAI) likely carries insurance; but who verifies? This is a coverage verification opportunity.
5. **$88B+ in construction starts in next 6 months** — Builder's risk market will be overwhelmed; opportunity to lock in deals early.
6. **Blackwell/Rubin GPU supply constrained** — Replacement time risk: if a GPU fails and can't be replaced for 18 months, business interruption exposure is enormous.
