## KEY TAKEAWAYS

1. **ZoomInfo, Apollo.io, RocketReach** are the dominant B2B data tools for prospecting insurance buyers. Apollo is specifically documented for commercial insurance lead gen workflows.
2. **Insurance-specific enrichment tools** (Archipelago, Cytora, SortSpoke, Veridion) address the submission/underwriting workflow — different from sales prospecting tools.
3. **GPU insurance is a real and growing specialty niche** — Marsh, Aon, WTW, Lloyd's already active. GPU Cost Hedging Insurance is a named market growing at 18.5% CAGR. Residual value insurance for GPU assets is supporting securitization deals.

### Associations Summary
1. **ELFA** is the most directly relevant association — members are actively financing GPU fleets (Wingspire's $65M deal). Target ELFA for distribution partnerships.
2. **UALink Consortium members** (AMD, AWS, Google, Meta, Microsoft, HPE, Intel, Apple, Cisco) represent the hyperscaler/GPU operator universe — the largest GPU fleet owners who need coverage.
3. **NVCA ecosystem** points to CoreWeave and Together AI as the GPU cloud infrastructure plays getting major VC backing — these are prime specialty insurance targets.
4. **ACG** is a relationship network play — mid-market PE firms funding AI infra buildouts need insurance solutions for their portfolio.
5. **AIIA (software-focused)** is less directly relevant but NVIDIA partnership signals GPU hardware involvement at edges.
