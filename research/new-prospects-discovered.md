## KEY STRATEGIC INSIGHTS

### 1. The Lender Mandation Strategy Is the Highest Leverage Play
**LuminArx, PIMCO, Blue Owl, Wingspire, Upper90** are all writing GPU-backed loans *right now*. If Corgi gets RVG written into their loan covenants as a condition of lending, every borrower in their portfolio becomes a customer automatically. One lender relationship = dozens of GPU operator clients. This is the highest-ROI approach.

### 2. The Crypto Miner Pivot Category Is Largely Uninsured
Companies like IREN (76,000 GB300s), Cipher Mining (600MW), Core Scientific (270MW), and Hut 8 are deploying GPU assets at extraordinary speed — faster than their risk management infrastructure can keep up. They came from a world where assets were fungible (Bitcoin miners are commodities). GPU hardware is not. These companies are likely underinsured on residual value.

### 3. Middle East = Highest Concentration of Unprotected GPU Risk
HUMAIN (1.9GW), Khazna (1GW+), DataVolt (1.5GW), Stargate UAE (5GW), Groq (3.5M LPUs) are all in new markets with *export control risk* — the US government could restrict chip exports at any time, stranding billions in planned purchases or forcing hardware substitution. RVG is particularly valuable in this environment. Also: these are all sovereign-backed entities with essentially unlimited capital and a need to demonstrate fiduciary rigor.

### 4. European Sovereign Cloud Is a Natural RVG Market
Evroc, Nscale, Taiga Cloud, Nebius, Nebul, Leaseweb, Exoscale — European operators are emphasizing *data sovereignty* which translates directly to *asset sovereignty*. GDPR and digital sovereignty regulation makes asset protection instruments more attractive. European insurers/reinsurers would also be more familiar buyers.

### 5. Alternative Silicon (Groq LPUs, SambaNova RDUs, Neuromorphic) Needs RVG Most
H100s have an established secondary market. LPUs, RDUs, and neuromorphic chips do not. Residual value risk is highest here — and this is exactly where RVG adds the most value. These companies should be priority prospects.
